Our Story

Tradition meets innovation

Saffron Spice brings the rich culinary heritage of India to Eluru. Founded in 2024, our restaurant combines traditional recipes passed down through generations with modern cooking techniques.

Every dish is prepared with love using the freshest ingredients and authentic spices sourced directly from local farmers. Our chefs have over 15 years of experience in fine dining restaurants across India.

We believe in creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate the diversity of Indian cuisine, from the fiery curries of the south to the creamy delicacies of the north.
